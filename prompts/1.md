We’re looking to reduce drudgery work at many companies that have people manually transferring data from papers

We want to create a proof of concept in which a person uploads several images of forms filled by hand and an AI transform the image into JSON that is saved in a database and stored in his account. The shape of the form will be known beforehand such that a certain schema is expected and the app displays an error if an image doesn’t match.

At this stage we want to skip authentication and database. We want the proof of concept to simulate and be a useful first step towards creating the real version. The app should use some form of local storage to run the demo.
